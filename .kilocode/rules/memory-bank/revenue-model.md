# Revenue Model for UXcaptain

This document outlines the recommended revenue model for UXcaptain based on comprehensive market research and competitor analysis.

## Overview

UXcaptain is a video-based user research platform focused on qualitative analytics for UX and product teams. The platform aims to make video-based user research as easy and scalable as sending a survey by providing features such as participant recruitment, video recording, and automated transcription.

## Recommended Revenue Model

Based on competitor analysis and UXcaptain's focus on qualitative analytics for UX/product teams, we recommend a tiered subscription model with the following structure (post-beta):

### Free Tier
- **Price:** €0/month
- **Features:** 
  - Unlimited studies
  - Unlimited own participants
  - Basic transcription with time stamps
  - Community support
  - No AI features
  - No demographic segments
  - Hosted in EU

### Starter Tier
- **Price:** €49/month
- **Features:**
  - Unlimited studies
  - Unlimited own participants
  - 5 participants included/month
  - Per-analysis entry transcription with AI
  - Full automated transcription
  - Priority email support with 24h response time
  - No demographic segments
  - Hosted in EU

### Pro Tier
- **Price:** €99/month
- **Features:**
  - Unlimited studies
  - Unlimited own participants
  - 10 participants included/month
  - Per-analysis entry transcription with AI
  - Demographic segments for participant targeting
  - Priority email support with 8h response time
  - Hosted in EU

## Competitive Positioning Strategy

1. **Against UXtweak**: Offer similar pricing but with access to participant pool and better transcription quality
2. **Against Userbrain**: Provide better value with more included features at similar price point
3. **Against Lookback**: Position as more affordable with reliable core functionality
4. **Against PlaybookUX**: Offer simpler, more accessible pricing without pay-per-use complexity

## Market Positioning

This positions UXcaptain as more affordable than enterprise-focused UserTesting while offering reliable core functionality compared to survey tools and European competitors. The tiered structure targets:

- **Small Teams (2-5 people)**: Starter and Pro tiers address the 45% of Spanish market that works in small teams
- **Sub-€1,000 Budgets**: Competitive pricing for the 35% of market that spends less than €1,000 annually on UX tools
- **European Market**: Local language support and GDPR compliance for European customers

## Revenue Model Advantages

1. **Predictable Recurring Revenue**: Subscription model provides consistent monthly/annual revenue
2. **Scalable Growth**: Tiered structure allows customers to upgrade as their needs grow
3. **Market Penetration**: Free tier attracts users who might later upgrade to paid tiers
4. **Transparent Pricing**: Clear, predictable pricing tiers unlike completely custom pricing of some competitors

## Implementation Considerations

1. **Beta Phase**: During beta, only the Beta tier will be offered
2. **Post-Beta Launch**: Transition to this 3-tier structure after beta period
3. **Feature Roadmap**:
   - Q1: Implement core features for all tiers
   - Q2: Add moderated session capabilities
   - Q3: Develop mobile testing features
   - Q4: Enhance integrations with popular UX tools
   - Future: Add AI features to differentiate tiers
4. **Pricing Adjustments**: Monitor competitor pricing changes and adjust annually based on market conditions and feature enhancements
5. **Customer Feedback**: Use customer feedback to refine pricing and features for future iterations

This revenue model leverages UXcaptain's core strengths in reliable video recording and transcription while providing a clear path to monetization across different market segments.
