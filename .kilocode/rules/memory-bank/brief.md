UXcaptain is a video-based user research platform designed for UX and product teams to record, analyze, and extract actionable insights from participant interactions with prototypes and products.

- Primary users: UX researchers, product managers, and designers looking to understand how users perceive heir products, designs, and experiences.
- Core jobs: Running unmoderated sessions, understanding where users struggle, and turning sessions into evidence for strategy, product, marketing and UX decisions.
- Key capabilities: Screen + audio recording & ad-hoc analysis creation.
