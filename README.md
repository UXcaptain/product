# UXcaptain

**What is UXcaptain?**
UXcaptain is a video-based user research platform designed to serve as the central hub for managing user research and insights within product departments. It automates transcription, analysis, and participant recruitment processes.

**Who are the potential users?**
Potential users include UX researchers, product managers, designers, and teams looking to understand how users perceive their products, designs, and experiences through unmoderated sessions efficiently.

**What this repo serves as:**
This repository acts as the "brain" of the product department by:

- Centralizing all user research activities.
- Automating processes such as writing PRDs, features & user stories, and other documentation.
- Providing actionable insights for informed decision-making.
- Supporting seamless integration with other tools within the product department.
